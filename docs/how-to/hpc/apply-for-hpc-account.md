# Apply for an HPC account

This guide explains how to request access to the UCT High Performance Computing (HPC) system.

---

## Before you start

You must:

- have an active UCT staff or student account  
- have a supervisor (Principal Investigator, PI) who can approve your request  

Third-party accounts are not permitted.

Before applying, read the HPC Acceptable Use Policy:

→ See: [Policies and service conditions](../../reference/hpc/policies-and-service-conditions.md)

---

## Application process

1. Log in to the UCT ServiceNow portal  
2. Submit an HPC account request  
3. Select your **correct PI account** from the dropdown list  
4. Your PI will receive an automated email requesting approval  
5. Your PI must:
   - respond from their UCT email account  
   - follow the approval instructions in the email  

Once your PI has approved the request, it is forwarded to the HPC team for account creation.

---

## Important notes

- Selecting your PI’s old or incorrect account will cause the application to fail  
- If your PI has email auto-forwarding enabled, approval may fail  
- If your PI does not respond within 2 weeks, the request will expire  

We recommend contacting your PI after submitting your request to ensure they have received the approval email.

---

## Access the application

Apply via the UCT ServiceNow portal.

If you see the message:

> "You are either not authorized or record is not valid."

log in using your UCT credentials (top right corner) and try again.

---

## After your account is created

You are expected to:

- comply with HPC usage policies  
- acknowledge the use of HPC infrastructure in publications  

→ See: [Policies and service conditions](../../reference/hpc/policies-and-service-conditions.md)
